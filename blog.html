About Our Restaurant

Located in the heart of the city, Spice & Soul offers an authentic taste of Pakistan. Our chefs bring traditional recipes passed down through generations, using spices and techniques that make each dish a celebration of culture and flavor.

What Makes Us Special

  • Freshly ground spices daily
  • Traditional Pakistani ambiance
  • Signature dishes like Biryani, Nihari, and Seekh Kebabs
  • Warm hospitality that makes you feel at home

Customer Favorites

  4. Falooda and Kulfi for dessert
