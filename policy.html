🍽️ Restaurant Customer Policy Document

📌 1. Introduction

Welcome to [Restaurant Name]! Our aim is to provide an exceptional dining experience to all guests. This policy outlines the rules and standards we uphold to ensure a comfortable, respectful, and enjoyable atmosphere for everyone.

📋 2. Reservation Policy

  • Reservation Required (Optional): Recommended for weekends and holidays.
  • Late Arrivals: Tables will be held for 15 minutes. After that, it may be released to other guests.
  • Group Bookings: Parties over 8 must pre-book and may require a deposit.

🧾 3. Dining Time Limit

  • Standard Dining Time: 90 minutes per table during peak hours.
  • Extended Seating: May be allowed based on availability or by request in advance.

🍽️ 4. Menu and Ordering

  • Special Dietary Needs: Please inform your server. We accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions wherever possible.
  • Last Order Time: Kitchen closes 30 minutes before official closing.
  • Outside Food/Beverages: Not permitted, except baby food or cakes with prior approval (cake-cutting fee may apply).

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 5. Conduct and Dress Code

  • Respectful Behavior: Disruptive or abusive behavior toward staff or guests will not be tolerated.
  • Dress Code: Casual dining attire is acceptable. No offensive or inappropriate clothing.
  • Children: Welcome under supervision. Please be mindful of noise and movement.

💳 6. Payment Policy

  • Accepted Payment Methods: Cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment apps.
  • Splitting Bills: Allowed up to 3 ways unless pre-arranged for larger parties.
  • Service Charge: A discretionary 10–15% service fee may be applied to groups of 6 or more.

🚭 7. Smoking and Alcohol Policy

  • Smoking: Prohibited inside. Designated outdoor areas available.
  • Alcohol: Served to patrons 21+ (valid ID required). Management reserves the right to refuse service if necessary.

🚫 8. Cancellation & No-Show Policy

  • Cancellations: Requested at least 4 hours in advance for regular bookings; 24 hours for large groups.
  • No-Show Fee: May apply for missed group reservations or peak-time bookings.

📸 9. Photography & Social Media

  • Photos: Allowed but avoid disturbing other diners. No flash photography.
  • Tag Us: Feel free to share your experience using #[RestaurantHandle].

📦 10. Takeout & Leftovers

  • Takeout Available: For select menu items. Call ahead or order online.
  • Leftovers: Packed upon request. Must be consumed at your own risk.

🔐 11. Privacy and Data Use

  • Customer Info: Used only for reservations and service purposes. We do not sell or misuse data.
  • Wi-Fi Use: Monitored to ensure legal and safe browsing.

🔚 12. Final Notes

  • Policy Updates: We reserve the right to amend this policy to ensure quality service and safety.
  • Feedback: We value your opinions. Please email feedback@restaurant.com or speak with a manager on-site.
